Welcome to LHS CyberSecurity Club

LHS Cyber Security Club is all about hacking, prevention, and much more. From learning how to use bash to the intricacies of the modern day World Wide Web, this club aims to be a blast for everyone involved.

Innovate

This club is unlike any other, and members will be encouraged to invent new ways to solve problems.

Enlighten

To gain valuable insight on cyber security, and protect against it. Be adaptable and master multiple Also, to think and code like a hacker.

Inspire

Inspire our club members to try new techniques in the world of computer security.
